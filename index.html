Open-source Frameworks

Using WordPress and WordPress frameworks can cut development costs by 50% or more. Using frameworks allow me to spend my time getting the details right like refining the user experience, and working on custom features. These frameworks aren’t just good because they’re open-source, in many cases they’re some of the best in the industry. WordPress itself powers a staggering 23% of the internet and has grown into a mature and flexible system that can power even a complex web business.

This Is What Democracy Looks Like

No other platform embodies the spirit of democratic, open source software more than WordPress. The platform was built for and by it's users and in the process catalyzed one of the largest do-it-yourself development communities in the world. This means if you ever have a question about WordPress, it's probably been asked and answered a hundred times already.
