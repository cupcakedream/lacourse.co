Well Built Web

My clients love me because I'm easy to work with and I do great work. I’m able to keep costs down by running a small shop and by working with technology that's affordable & open-source. From your brand to your next great idea, you can trust it will be developed thoughtfully and practically.

Open-source Frameworks

WordPress powers a staggering 23% of the internet and has grown into a mature and flexible framework that can power even a complex web business. Most WordPress extensions are affordable if not open-source and can sometimes cut the development costs on big projects up to 50-75%. Building on top of existing infastructure allows me to spend my time getting the details right, creating a satisfying, effective user experience.

No other platform embodies the spirit of democratic, open source software more than WordPress. The platform was built by the people, for the people, and in the process catalyzed the largest do-it-yourself community in the world. That means if you ever have a question about WordPress, it's probably been asked and answered a hundred times over.

What you get

You'll get a beautiful, fast, reliable web site coded and tested to work on mobile, tablet, and desktop resolutions as well as all the major browsers and operating systems. For most projects, I ask for half up front to begin work and half once the project is approved and uploaded to your server.
